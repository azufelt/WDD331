Animated Note Cards

Ashley Zufelt, CSS wdd331 Week 10

List of card transition ideas:

  1. card flips from front to back
  2. Card slides in from the side
  3. Cut strips slide in from opposites sides
  4. Drops from top with a bounce
  5. Multiple layers that flatten and turn
  6. A circle that spins and corners unfold to card
  7. Unfolds from an Origami shape
  8. Square that unfolds hamburger, then hotdog
  9. Starts from speck, grows to card size with a wiggle
  10. Fan out a hand of cards
  11. Gets larger when click to type
  12. Up/down curtain reveals card
  13. Wiggles
  14. Checkerboard puzzle pieces flip to reveal card
  15. Confetti falls from top
  16. Line zips horizontal, then grows box big, like turning on/off TV
  17. Box shakes and tiles fall off to reveal card
  18. Accordion folds open
  19. Crumbled ball unfolds to card
  20. Sides of page are torn away to reveal card

Fanning Cards

  • Make multiple cards
  • Give them all absolute position, so they are stacked on top of each other
  • animate them to rotate, giving them differing rotate degreees so they appear "fanned" out
  • set the "transform-origin" attribute, so they look like they are spreading from being held in a hand or a ring keeper

Unfolding Card

  • Start with small square position will remain absolute
  • Double width of square, forming a "hotdog" (long and skinny) rectangle
  • Double height of rectangle, making both width & height now equal
